比较下面8个软件，结论：AllPATHS-LG性能最优，组装的contig和scaffold比较长，错误率低；SOAP denovo组装得到的contig和scaffold几乎是几个软件里最长的，但是错误率也相对较高！   ABySS (Simpson et al. 2009) • ALLPATHS-LG (Gnerre et al. 2011) • Bambus2 (Koren et al. 2011) (http://www.cbcb.umd.edu/software/ bambus). • CABOG (Miller et al. 2008) • MSR-CA (http://www.genome.umd.edu/MSR\_CA\_MANUAL.htm) • SGA (Simpson and Durbin 2012) • SOAPdenovo (Li et al. 2010b) • Velvet (Zerbino and Birney 2008) ———————————— http://gage.cbcb.umd.edu/index.html

What is GAGE?

GAGE is an evaluation of the very latest large-scale genome assembly algorithms. We have organized this “bake-off” as an attempt to produce a realistic assessment of genome assembly software in a rapidly changing field of next-generation sequencing. The main results of GAGE have now been published in the journal Genome Research: GAGE: A critical evaluation of genome assemblies and assembly algorithms.
